How to Make an Iced Pumpkin Cream Chai Tea Latte

Ingrediants Needed:

Chai tea concentrate: (Tazo Chai Tea Concentrate or simply the brand you like best.)
2% milk (for chai tea and for cream)
Heavy cream
Vanilla simple syrup: you can make your own or just buy it.
Sweetened condensed milk
Pumpkin puree
Pumpkin pie spice
Vanilla extract

How to make Pumpkin Spice Sauce:

How to make Pumpkin Spice Sauce

How to make Pumpkin Cream Cold Foam:

How to make Pumpkin Cream Cold Foam

Finally, it's time to put it all together!

First, add chai tea concentrate to an ice-filled glass.
Then, pour milk into glass.
Then, top drink with pumpkin cream cold foam. Finally, sprinkle some pumpkin spice sauce to the top.
And there you have it! A Pumpkin Cream Chai Tea Latte! Hope you all enjoy!
